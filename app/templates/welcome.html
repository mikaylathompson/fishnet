Welcome to fishnet

Fishnet is a site for catching and sharing links.

At it's heart, it's bookmarks with annotations: write a note about why you're saving the link, what parts of the page you want to reference, a particularly important quote, or just a summary.

Links are saved in folders, which you can share with others. Your user page shows all of your folders and links to provide a snapshot of what you're reading or thinking about.

Beyond bookmarks, you could make a portfolio folder with links to your projects, or put together a curated reading list about a particular topic, or keep track of articles you've read and your thoughts on them. It's up to you!

To get started with fishnet, login or register.
